These are the pros and cons to fencing with Foils, Epees, and Sabres.

Fencing Style Pros Cons
Foil It is very rewarding and it is the funnest of all fencing styles. It requires more skill than epee or sabre does because of the right of way rule and the smaller target area.
Epee It is very easy to pick up and since there is no right of way rule, it is much easier than foil. Since it is much easier, you don't work as hard.
Sabre It is a slashing-like sport so many consider it fun even though you are just waving around a block of metal. It requires much more control than epee but not as much as foil

(Due to the fact that the author of this website is a foil fencer, please keep in mind that all information is biased.)
